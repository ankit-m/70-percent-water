About

70percentwater.org is a unique initiative that seeks to support water equity and water conservation through unique, people-powered campaigns that are designed to maximise water conservation with smartly modified designs of everyday urban habits, products & techniques.

In this process, water-rich areas end up supporting projects in water deficit regions, leading to water equity.

Problem

Earth’s irony is that on a planet that is 70% covered with water, two-thirds of the global population live under conditions of severe water scarcity at least 1 month of the year.

By 2025, nearly two billion people will be living in countries or regions with absolute water scarcity according to the United Nations.

70percentwater.org is a community of conscious people around the world addressing the problem of water equity and scarcity, before it’s too late.
